2-Day Agra Adventure Itinerary

Experience the best of Agra in just two days, filled with history, culture, and adventure under ₹5000!

Day 1: Exploring Agra’s History and Heritage

Morning

  • Arrival in Agra (Budget: ₹400 for round trip)
  • Breakfast at a local dhaba (Budget: ₹100-₹150)
  • Visit the Taj Mahal (₹50 for Indians)

Afternoon

  • Explore Agra Fort (₹50)
  • Lunch at a local restaurant (Budget: ₹200)
  • Visit Mehtab Bagh (₹25), Camel Ride/ e-bike tour (₹300-₹400)

Evening

  • Stroll at Sadar Bazaar, try local food (₹150)
  • Stay at a budget guesthouse (₹700-₹1000)

Day 2: Local Culture and Adventure

Morning

  • Breakfast (₹100)
  • Visit Fatehpur Sikri (₹50) - Shared cab/bus (₹150-₹200)

Afternoon

  • Return to Agra for Lunch (₹200)
  • Yamuna River Boat Ride (₹200-₹300)

Evening

  • Taj Nature Walk (₹20)
  • Enjoy sunset at a rooftop café (₹150)
  • Return journey (₹400)


Estimated Budget: ₹4000-₹5000
