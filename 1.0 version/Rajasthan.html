Day 1: Arrival and Exploring Jaipur

Morning

  • Arrival in Jaipur (Budget: ₹300-500 for Sleeper Class)
  • Check-in (Budget: ₹900-2000 per night.)
  • Visit the Hawa Mahal(₹50 for Indians)
  • Visit the City Palace(₹200 for Indians)

Afternoon

  • Explore Amer Fort (₹100 for Indians)
  • Head to a local restaurant for a thali meal for ₹200-300
  • Visit Mehtab Bagh (₹25), Camel Ride/ e-bike tour (₹300-₹400)

Evening

  • Visit Jal Mahal(Free Entry)
  • Dinner: Opt for a budget-friendly local restaurant or street food, costing around ₹150-250

Day 2: Local Culture and Adventure

Morning

  • Breakfast (₹100)
  • Visit Nahargarh Fort (₹50 for Indians)
  • Jaipur’s Markets: Johari Bazaar & Bapu Bazaar (Free Entry)

Afternoon

  • Albert Hall Museum: The museum houses an extensive collection of artifacts, paintings, and sculptures.(₹200 for Indians)
  • Yamuna River Boat Ride (₹200-₹300)

Evening

  • Return journey (₹300-₹500 Sleeper Class )


Estimated Budget: ₹6500-₹8000
