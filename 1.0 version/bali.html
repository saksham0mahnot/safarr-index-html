Day 1: Arrival in Bali

Morning

  • Transfer to Ubud: Hire a private taxi or a shared transfer (₹1,500 - ₹2,000).
  • Check-in at a Budget-Friendly Ubud Homestay (₹3,000 - ₹5,000 per night)
  • Visit the Hawa Mahal(₹50 for Indians)
  • Visit Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary: Explore the lush forest filled with playful monkeys and ancient temples (₹500 entry fee).

Afternoon

  • Lunch at a Local Warung:Try traditional Indonesian dishes like Nasi Goreng or Mie Goreng.(₹300 - ₹500)
  • Visit Ubud Palace and Saraswati Temple: Ubud Palace is a great place to explore Balinese architecture and cultural significance (Free)
  • Stroll through Ubud Art Market for local crafts and souvenirs (Budget ₹500 - ₹1,000).

Evening

  • Tegallalang Rice Terraces (Free)
  • Dinner at a Warung or Budget Restaurant (~₹500 - ₹1,000).

Night

  • Traditional Balinese Dance Performance at Ubud Palace (₹300 - ₹500)

Day 2: Beaches and Temples in South Bali

Morning

  • Check-out from Ubud and Head to South Bali (₹1,500 - ₹2,000 for transportation)
  • Visit Uluwatu Temple (₹300 entry fee)
  • Enjoy the Beaches: Visit Padang Padang Beach (~₹150 entry fee)

Afternoon

  • Lunch at a Local Beach Café; Enjoy fresh seafood or local dishes by the beach. (₹500 - ₹700)
  • Visit GWK Cultural Park: See the enormous Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue, and explore the park's cultural displays. (₹400 entry fee).

Evening

  • Catch the Kecak Fire Dance at Uluwatu Temple: Witness the stunning sunset while watching the famous Kecak dance. (₹500 entry fee)
  • Dinner at Jimbaran Beach: A romantic seafood dinner on the beach, offering a wonderful end to the day (₹1,500 - ₹2,000)

Night

  • Stay in Kuta/Legian (Budget-friendly accommodation: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 per night).
  • Enjoy Bali's nightlife in Kuta or just relax after a packed day.


Estimated Budget: ₹20000-₹30000
