Day 1: Explore Bangkok

Morning: Arrival in Bangkok

  • Flight (Budget: ₹12000-15000 for Round Trip)
  • Accommodation (Budget: ₹1000-1500 per night.)
  • The Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew (500 THB)
  • Lunch: Opt for street food stalls or small restaurants near the palace.(Around 40-60 THB)

Afternoon

  • Wat Pho (Temple of the Reclining Buddha) (200 THB)
  • Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn) (100 THB)

Evening

  • Khao San Road: Famous for its nightlife, street markets, and budget shopping
  • Dinner: Enjoy street food, such as skewers, mango sticky rice, and spring rolls (50-100 THB).
  • Nightlife: Budget-friendly bars with drinks for around 100-150 THB.

Day 2: Day Trip to Ayutthaya or Floating Markets

Morning

  • Breakfast (₹100)
  • Ayutthaya Day Trip: (Entry Fee: 50 thb)
  • Ayutthaya Historical Park, Wat Phra Si Sanphet, Wat Mahathat (famous for the Buddha head entwined in tree roots).

Afternoon

  • Lunch: Local eateries or street food (around 50-80 THB per meal).
  • Visit Floating Markets : Explore local markets where vendors sell fruits, snacks, and souvenirs from boats.

Evening

  • Relaxation or Shopping
  • Visit MBK Mall or Terminal 21 for affordable souvenirs and shopping.
  • Dinner: Street food or budget eateries in Sukhumvit (around 100-150 THB).


Estimated Budget: ₹19000-₹20000
